Thoughts on Academia

Wed 13 June 2012 by Hugo Shi

Those of you who know me, know that I spent 5 years in Academia, and then got out! I actually had a pretty good deal in academia, I wasn't hounded by my department, my advisor was fantastic, I was always well-enough funded (by grad school standards) But i've gotta say, leaving was one of the best things I ever did.
